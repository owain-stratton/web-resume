Hi there, my name is Owain and I'm a co-founder of Geoshepherds, a geo design consultancy based in Sweden. After completing a B.Sc. in Geography I broadened my industry knowledge and technical skills working as a satellite imagery specialist in Australia. Currently I'm creating geospatial data visualisations for web and mobile platforms, and enjoying myself in the process. I have a great interest in technology and how it can be utilised to communicate data effectively.

A little bit more about me; I enjoy work that challenges me, I like to take a pragmatic approach to problem-solving, I'm curious about the technical aspects to how and why things work, and the little details always grab my attention.
